The Design of
Undestanding
2016

The Design of Understanding is a one day conference looking at how ideas are designed to be more understandable. We look within and beyond the discipline of information design to related fields: this year with speakers from government, product design, comics, toy makers, and magazines.

It is hosted by the Welcome Library and curated by Max Gadney, founder of After the Flood, an information design consultancy.
